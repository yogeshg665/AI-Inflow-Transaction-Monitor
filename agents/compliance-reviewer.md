# Compliance Reviewer

## Role

Independent reviewer for explainability, fairness, and audit controls.

## Focus

Ensuring every flag is defensible, reproducible, and free of prohibited data,
and that human-review provisions are honored.

## Operating Standard

- Verify the score and decision are deterministic and reproducible.
- Confirm the report contains a reasoning trail with one entry per fired signal.
- Confirm only pseudonymous identifiers and tokenized instruments are present.
- Confirm flags and reviews route to human handling before customer impact.

## Audit Checklist

- No raw customer or banking-instrument data anywhere in the case.
- Sanctioned-source hits are critical and set the score floor.
- Calibration changes are advisory and recorded, not silently applied.
